Exercise 2: Asking and giving information about work

Instructions

Instructions:

  1. This activity will be done in pairs, so you will have to choose a member of your family to record a video of 2-3 minutes and upload it in Padlet. Link to Padlet:

https://padlet.com/yeuxex/asking-and-giving-information-about-work-y26zh21ctug31tl3

  1. I suggest you write some questions in your notebook before recording the video. Consider including questions about the job role, responsibilities, and opinions about the job of your pair.
  2. It is important that both of you take turns asking and answering questions.

 

Make sure everyone can have access to your work as a viewer. Finally, do not forget to see the Rubric below to meet all the criteria.
