Jobs

Jobs vocabulary

Vocabulary bank

Nurse

A person who works in a hospital helping patients when they don't feel good, and they need treatment. They work with doctors and administrate medicaments to patients.

Doctor

It is someone who works at a hospital helping sick people and treating illnesses. These people have a degree in medicine, and they have a wide knowledge of it.

Accountant

A person that works makes accounting functions, administrates, and analyzes the incomes and taxes.

Office worker

These people do mostly administrative work in an office. They work in a desk using a computer administrating documents.

Cashier

Is the person that works with money charging costumers in a store for the products they buy.

Teacher

A person who works with children, teenagers, or adults at school. Teachers plan a class to make their students learn something new.

Police officer

A person who maintain order in the city, arrest criminals and ensures safety of people

Receptionist

The person who greets visitors as they walk into an office or building, or hotel.

Bellhop

Someone employed as an errand boy and luggage carrier around hotels.

Sales person

A person who sales a product or service giving information to people interested in it.

Server (Waiter / waitress)

A person who takes order form clients in a restaurant, gives the menu and take the dishes to the table.

Taxi driver

They drive people to their destinations when they are asked for a ride.

Unusual jobs

Dog food tester

A person who works testing food prepared specificly for dogs. These people test dog's food before selling it.

Line stander

Sometimes, people don't like waiting much time to make something or request for documents. Line standers are hired to do this job and wait in the line for someone else.

Ticket counter

These people work in a specific place selling tickets for trains or other means of transports. They can sell a ticket for an arriving train or book it for later.

Volcano observer

They work watching and monitoring volcano activity for scientific analysis. They advise people when it is safe to go and alert for possible risks in case of volcano eruption.

Artist

These people are into arts. An artist creates amazing art pieces like paintings, dance, theather,etc. Some pieces of art like these become popular and are exhibited in museums, presentations, etc.
