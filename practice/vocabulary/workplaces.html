Jobs vocabulary

Vocabulary bank

Hospital

An institution of medical and surgical treatment and nursing care for sick or injured people.

Office

A place to work with desktops where people work with computers doing administrative work.

Hotel

A place were guests can host in it. People who work in hotels greet and help guests to be comfortable during their stay.

Restaurant

A place where people go to have meals. They can order food to a waiter there.

School

An educational institution where children go to learn.

Store

It is a shop that sells products of a specific departament (clothing, convenience, furniture, electronic).
