Listening space

Listening practice 1

In the following exercise, you will listen to six different people talking about what they do in their job. From the description they give, guess what each of them does for a living.

Transcript

Transcript 

Speaker 1: Hello! I am Elizabeth. My days are filled with patient care, administering medications, and collaborating with doctors to esure our patients receive the best treatment possible. It is a fast-paced environment, but making a positive impact in someone's health is truly rewarding.

Speaker 2: My name is Adrian. My job involves maintaining public safety, responding to emergencies, and working closely with the community to build trust. I keep people safe and help when there are problems.

Speaker 3: Hey there! I am Daniel. I drive people to places they need to go. I like meeting new people and chatting during the ride. Hearing their stories is what makes this job interesting. I pride myself on getting people to their destinations safely. 

Speaker 4: Hi everyone! I am Marshall. My days are spent in the classroom, teaching mathematics to 10th-grade students. It can be hard, but it is great when students understand and learn new things.

Speaker 5: Greetings! I am Erika. I work with a variety of animals, from cats and dogs to exotic pets. My job involves diagnosing abd treating illnesses, performing surgeries, performing taking care of our furry friends. I make sure they are healthy.

Speaker 6: I am Aaron. Flying has been a dream since I was a child, and now I travel all over the world, ensuring passengers reach their destinations safely and comfortably. It is exciting to travel and see new places. Making sure everyone has a safe flight is my job.

Task 1: What is your job?

Task: True or false.

Listen to six people talking about their work. After listening to each of the descriptions, choose the correct answer.
