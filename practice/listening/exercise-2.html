Listening space

Exercise 2

In the following exercise, you will listen to five different conversations of people catching up on where they work. Pay attention to the workplaces mentioned.

Transcript

Conversation 1

Chris: Long time, no see, George! Where do you work now?

George: I work at a restaurant as a chef. The kitchen is my world. How about you, Chris?

Chris: Hmmm, I manage a grocery store now. Making sure everything's in order.

Conversation 2

John: Good to catch up, Mike! What is your job these days?

Mike: I am at the supermarket; I manage the cash registers and help customers. You, John?

John: That is great. I handle finances at the bank. It gets busy, but I enjoy it.

Conversation 3

Paul: How is life treating you, Anna? Where do you work now?

Anna: I am at the local post office. I sort and deliver mails. How about you, Paul?

Paul: I am part of the hotel staff at The Grand Horizon. Guests keep things interesting.

Conversation 4

Noah: Hey, Ryan! It's been a while. What do you do for a living now?

Ryan: I work at the airport as a security guard. Quite a change, right? How about you, Noah?

Noah I still at the café, brewing coffee and serving smiles. Come by for a cup!

Conversation 5

Lily: Wow! It's been ages, Tyler. What are you up to these days?

Tyler: I work at a school as an English teacher. I love teaching. And you, Lily?

Lily: I still in the hospital scene. I am always busy, but I love it.

Task 1: Where do you work?

Listen carefully to five different conversations where workplaces are mentioned. Which workplaces are mentioned?
