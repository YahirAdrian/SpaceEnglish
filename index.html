Topics

Find the topic you need from this topic library. You can find some exercises and explanations about each topic, acompained with skills practice to reinforce your knowledge.

Jobs

What is your profession?

In this section you will learn common job vocabulary and phrases in English. Listening to recordings of people talking about their everyday work responsibilities. Read about job experiences and take quizzes on job-related vocabulary and basic expressions.

View topic

Routines

What is your routine?

In this section you will read and listen to native speakers describing their typical daily routines and schedules. Also, you will practice describing your own typical routine using simple sentence structures.

View topic

Describing food

Build your English food glossary through varied tasting experiences!

Listen to native speakers order meals and describe how dishes taste, look and smell and practice expressing your own preferences about different foods using simple constructed responses.

View topic

Vacation plans

It's time to visit a new country!

Get exposure to basic travel English vocabulary involving transportation, lodging, money, and directions. Study real-world conversations about vacations and tips for travelers and listen to recordings of people discussing their travel plans or experiences.
