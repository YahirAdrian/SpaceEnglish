About us

Our project

Space English

"It's not rocket science"

Space English is an educational website project created to help students who want to learn English by practicing all their skills regarding to a specific topic. We want to make sure that all the content you practice here can be easy for you to use and forget about spending hard time learning with a traditional mode with books or printed resources.

As our slogan says, “It’s not rocket science”, we want you to see that learning English is not a difficult task. So, you can enjoy learning English with interactive activities. Another thing is that you can check other’s work in sections like writing and speaking, that will allow you to guide in the process in practicing by seeing others’ work.
